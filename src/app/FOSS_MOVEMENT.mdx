  Key Values and Principles
</Heading>

<KeyValue
  number="1"
  text="Move from #commercial-software to #free-open-source-software, wherever possible"
>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Motivation">
    Porsche empowers their employees to dedicate time for participating in FOSS
    activities.
  </Principle>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS prefer">
    An employee should evaluate open and inner-source software before deciding
    for commercial software.
  </Principle>
</KeyValue>
<KeyValue
  number="2"
  text="Move from #consume to #contribute and #create in open-source-communities"
>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Contributions">
    FOSS activities are not only limited to contributing code but should also
    embrace non-code contributions such as design, translations, bug reporting,
    UX Improvement, recruiting, testing, documentation, project and community
    management, communication and many more.
  </Principle>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Collaboration">
    An employee should be committed and active in inner-source and open-source
    communities to foster collaboration, build software faster, and embrace
    innovation.
  </Principle>
</KeyValue>
<KeyValue number="3" text="Move from #know-it-all to #learn-it-all">
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Community">
    Porsche shall support and enable its employees to use, contribute to,
    influence, and launch new FOSS activities to acquire knowledge and partner
    with technology leaders.
  </Principle>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Ethics">
    An employee should always act responsibly, adhere to existing codes of
    conduct, and own their FOSS activities to endorse a positive image.
  </Principle>
</KeyValue>
<KeyValue
  number="4"
  text="Move towards #digital-transformation with open-source striving for speed of #innovation and shorter time to market"
>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Visibility">
    Porsche contributions in open-source communities should establish a
    foundation to promote reputation as digitally enabled company and attract IT
    experts.
  </Principle>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Reusability">
    An employee should be open to share and consume any FOSS activity within the
    Porsche inner-source community to achieve shorter time to market by „not
    reinventing the wheel“.
  </Principle>
</KeyValue>
<KeyValue
  number="5"
  text="Move towards a #compliant environment for FOSS activities and #contributions"
>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Integrity">
    Porsche provides all means and information to enable the responsible
    employee to act in full awareness of the “two sides of one coin” and avoid
    unnecessary risks for Porsche and the employees: the benefits of FOSS
    activities on one side, and the corresponding legal, tax and business
    requirements on the other side.
  </Principle>
  <Principle hashtag="FOSS Compliance">
    An employee should be encouraged and empowered to use FOSS and contribute to
    upstream projects by being provided with lean but compliant processes.
  </Principle>
</KeyValue>
